Gang Box: GB-300 and GB-302

Exacta Plastics makes Gang Boxes for temporary power distribution systems. They are made from PVC material and black in color for UV resistance. Gang Boxes come with standard Edison plate or GFI square receptable plate. GB-300 has one hole for the cable passage and the GB-302 has two holes for cable passage.
